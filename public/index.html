I’m a physicist turned visual storyteller and computational wizard, crafting stunning animations and physics-based simulations. With a strong foundation in physics and years of research and industry experience, I love merging science and art to build immersive 3D worlds. Whether simulating plasma particles through code, rigging robots in Maya, or implementing physics-based animation in Omniverse, I turn complex ideas into compelling visual stories. Dive in and explore where imagination meets code and becomes reality.
